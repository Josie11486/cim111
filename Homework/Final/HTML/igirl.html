BEAUTY REVIEW: MY TOP 5 MAKEUP PRODUCTS

May 2, 2018

Intro

1. GWEN STEFANI

Gwen Stefani is the first real artist I listened to after graduating from Kidz Bop. Me and my mom went to see her live when I was about 7. She was on the Love Angel Music Baby tour and it was amazing! That is probably still my favorite Gwen album, but I love Sweet Escape too. She has made less music than the other women on this list, but she’s an icon and she’s been with me from the beginning, so I had to include her.

2. RIHANNA

Rihanna has only gotten better with time, but I was a fan all the way back when SOS and Pon De Replay were her biggest hits. It was really fun to watch her progress over the years. Today she’s one of the best selling artists in the world, she is a style icon, and she has her own makeup and lingerie lines. What’s not to love? 

3. KEHLANI

Kehlani is definitely the newest artist on this list, but she had exploded on to the music scene and she’s killing it. Lani has the sweetest singing voice but she can rap too. I think her album’s name “Sweet Sexy Savage” is the perfect description.

4. NICKI MINAJ

Nicki really brings her personality into her music. She has a few alter egos and they all have a different style. She’s probably the best female rapper today but she was just as good when she was less famous. From Itty Bitty Piggy to Chun Li, she always brings the heat.

5. BEYONCE

Of course Queen B had to be on here. She has been a big deal since Destiny’s Child and she hasn’t slowed down since. Bey is her own competition, and she outdoes herself every time. I would say more but I think she speaks for herself.
