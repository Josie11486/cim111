APPRECIATION POST: THE VALUE OF MEMES

April 19, 2018
The Google dictionary defines a meme as “an element of a culture or system of behavior that may be considered to be passed from one individual to another by non-genetic means, especially imitation” and “a humorous image, video, piece of text, etc., that is copied (often with slight variations) and spread rapidly by Internet users.”

To most people, memes are just silly jokes, but they are pretty interesting when you look at them as a reflection of culture. Memes can both reflect and influence different aspects of culture.

Language: Memes can play a big role in popularizing slang words or phrases, for example:

"pupper" and "doggo"

"lit"

"shoot your shot"

"cash me outside"

Political opinions: there are countless political memes, but just becuase they're "jokes" doesn't mean they don't reflect real opinons or have an effect on voters.


The New Yorker's political cartoons are taken more seriously, but essentially do the same thing.
