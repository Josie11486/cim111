Chicago

Cities like New York and Las Vegas used to attract the most tourism in the US, but over the past few years, Chicago has become an increasingly popular travel destination. The Windy City has lots to offer- amazing architecture, a plethora of great restaurants, historical landmarks, and a beautiful waterfront to name a few.
