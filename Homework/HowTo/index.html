How To: Lemon Cake

Step 1: Gather Ingredients

    Cake

  • 2 x 8in tins
  • 6 eggs
  • 12oz self raising flour
  • 12oz unsalted butter
  • 12oz caster sugar (granulated)
  • 1tsp baking powder
  • Juice + zest 2 lemons
  • Splash of milk

    Frosting:

  • 250g cream cheese
  • 50g butter (room temp)
  • 100g Icing/powdered sugar
  • Juice + zest one lemon

Step 2: Pre-Heat the Oven

350 Degrees

Butter Your Pan

Make sure to coat the bottom and the sides thuroughly.

Mix Ingredients

Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder first. Then add eggs, butter, milk, and lemons.

Bake

Put your cake in the oven for about 30 minutes, or until they're no longer pale and wobbly. If you're not sure whether it's ready, stick a tooth pick in the center of the cake. If it comes out clean, they're done!

Enjoy!

Best served with a fresh cup of coffee!
